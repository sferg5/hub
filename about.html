About

Welcome to your Motion Specialist Hub

One home for all your motion work.

This is your personal command center — a place to plan projects, track your time and capacity, collect requests from stakeholders, and showcase finished work. Everything lives in one connected space and stays in sync across your devices. New here? The quick tour below shows what each area does and how to get started.

What's inside

The six areas of the hub

Jump between them anytime from the menu in the top-left corner — it's on every page.

Dashboard

Your full command center. Track every project across Kanban, Cards, Timeline, and Calendar views; plan your week with a capacity-aware daily briefing; log time; and keep links, notes, a daily checklist, and analytics all in one place.

Password-protected

Quick View

A clean, read-only snapshot that's safe to share or glance at — at-a-glance stats, this week's work, upcoming time off, recently delivered projects, and your daily checklist.

Motion Library

A visual showcase of your completed work. Each entry leads with its image or video, and expands to reveal collaborators, links, and notes — building a living portfolio as you ship.

Motion Tools

A home for the interactive tools you've built, including the User Flow Template for storyboarding flows step by step. Add your own tools with previews, links, and descriptions.

Intake Form

A public request form. Share the link with stakeholders so they can submit motion requests — with project type, priority, and details — that flow straight into your tracker.

Data Manager

Export, import, and sync your data. Save a backup to your repo and load it back on any device, so your hub travels with you and nothing is ever lost.

Getting started

Your first few minutes

1

Open the Dashboard and add a project

Head to the Dashboard and create your first project — or, if you've used the hub before, import your saved data from the Data Manager to pick up where you left off.

2

Plan your week in the Daily Briefing

The briefing balances your available hours against what's on your plate and flags time off, so you can shape a realistic week at a glance.

3

Share the Intake Form with stakeholders

Send the Intake Form link to anyone who needs motion work. Their requests arrive with the context you need and land right in your tracker.

4

Showcase finished work in the Motion Library

As projects ship, add them to the Library with a hero image or video. Over time it becomes a polished, browsable portfolio of your craft.

5

Share status with Quick View

Point teammates to Quick View for a read-only snapshot of where things stand — no risk of accidental edits.

Good to know

A few helpful details

It syncs automatically

Your data is saved and kept in sync across devices, so the hub looks the same whether you open it on your laptop or your phone.

Public vs. private

The Dashboard and Data Manager are password-protected. The Intake Form, Quick View, Library, and Tools are safe to share with others.

The menu is everywhere

The hamburger menu in the top-left corner appears on every page — it's the fastest way to move between the six areas of the hub.
